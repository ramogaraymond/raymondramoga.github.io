Microsoft Excel Dashboard

HR Dataset | HR Analytics Dashboard

Welcome to my Microsoft Excel Dashboard , where I demonstrate my proficiency in harnessing the capabilities of Excel to derive valuable insights using the HR dataset

As a data analyst committed to leveraging data for informed decision-making, I have meticulously developed three comprehensive dashboards: the Employee Comprehensive Overview Dashboard, the Performance and Absenteeism Dashboard, and the Attrition Dashboard.

#1 EMPLOYEE COMPREHENSIVE DASHBOARD:

This dashboard offers a holistic view of employee-related metrics , providing key stakeholders with a comprehensive overview of total hired employees, active employees, and average salary . Through intuitive visualizations and dynamic charts, users can explore employee data such as age group, gender distribution, employee performance and status, total employees per department, top recruitment source and hiring trend, facilitating strategic workforce planning and talent management initiatives.

#2 PERFORMANCE AND ABSENTEEISM DASHBOARD:

Focused on tracking employee performance and attendance patterns, the Performance and Absenteeism Dashboard enables organizations to identify trends and patterns impacting productivity. By analyzing metrics such as employee engagement, average absences , and total absences, this dashboard empowers managers and HR professionals to pinpoint areas for improvement, implement targeted interventions, and optimize workforce efficiency.

#3 ATTRITION DASHBOARD:

Addressing the critical challenge of employee attrition, the Attrition Dashboard provides insights into the factors influencing employee turnover and retention . Through detailed analysis of attrition trends, attrition rate, and employee satisfaction , organizations can gain valuable insights into the drivers of employee churn, allowing them to implement proactive retention strategies, mitigate talent loss, and foster a positive workplace culture.

Power BI Dashboard

Adventure Works Dashboard | Dataset

Welcome to my Power BI Dashboard, where I showcase my expertise in leveraging this powerful business intelligence tool to provide valuable insights using the Adventure Works dataset.

As a data analyst committed to delivering actionable insights, I have meticulously crafted three comprehensive dashboards: the Executive Sales Dashboard , the Sales Map, and the Customer Detail Dashboard.

#1 EXECUTIVE SALES DASHBOARD:

This dashboard is tailored for executives and decision-makers , offering a high-level overview of sales performance across various dimensions such as regions, products, and time periods. By visually presenting key metrics such as total revenue, profit, profit margin, total orders, top-selling products, etc. This dashboard empowers stakeholders to make informed strategic decisions swiftly and effectively.

#2 SALES MAP:

The Sales Map dashboard harnesses the power of geographical visualization to provide a spatial understanding of sales distribution. Through interactive maps and intuitive visualizations, users can explore sales data geographically, identifying total orders by regions, spotting country with highest sales, and uncovering potential opportunities or challenges across different geographic locations.

#3 CUSTOMER DETAIL DASHBOARD:

Delving deeper into customer analytics, the Customer Detail Dashboard offers a granular view of customer behavior, preferences, and interactions.
